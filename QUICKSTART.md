# Quick Start Guide

## First Time Setup

### 1. Launch the App
```bash
npm run dev
```

### 2. Create Your Account
- Tap "Sign Up" on the login screen
- Enter your email and password (minimum 6 characters)
- Confirm your password
- Tap "Sign Up"

### 3. Set Initial Balance
- Enter your current account balance (money in bank)
- Enter your current cash in hand
- Tap "Continue"
- You're all set!

## Add Your First Transaction

### Recording an Expense
1. Tap the **"Add"** tab
2. Select **"Expense"**
3. Enter amount (e.g., 50)
4. Select category (e.g., "Food")
5. Choose payment method:
   - **Account**: if paid via bank/card
   - **Cash**: if paid with cash in hand
6. Add optional description (e.g., "Lunch at cafe")
7. Tap **"Add Transaction"**

Your balance will automatically update!

### Recording Income
1. Same steps but select **"Income"**
2. Choose income category (Salary, Gift, etc.)
3. Money is added to your selected method

## View Your Data

### Dashboard (Home Tab)
- See your total balance
- View breakdown of account vs. cash
- Browse recent transactions

### Calendar (Calendar Tab)
- Visual month view
- Red dots = expenses, Green dots = income
- Click a date to see transactions for that day
- Edit or delete transactions

### Profile (Profile Tab)
- See daily/monthly/yearly statistics
- View top spending categories
- Get AI spending suggestions

## Common Tasks

### Check Monthly Expenses
1. Go to **Profile** tab
2. Look at **"This Month"** section
3. See total expenses and income

### Edit Past Transaction
1. Go to **Calendar** tab
2. Navigate to the month
3. Tap the date with transaction
4. Tap **Edit** icon on the transaction
5. Change amount or description
6. Tap **Update**

### Get Spending Advice
1. Go to **Profile** tab
2. Tap **"Get AI Spending Suggestions"**
3. Read personalized recommendations

### Update My Balances
1. Go to **Profile** tab
2. Tap **Edit** icon next to balances
3. Enter new account and cash amounts
4. Tap **Update**

## Tips

- **Be Consistent**: Record transactions daily for accurate insights
- **Use Descriptions**: Helps you remember why you spent money
- **Correct Categories**: Improves AI suggestions
- **Regular Reviews**: Check profile weekly to monitor spending
- **Update Manually**: If balances get out of sync with actual money
